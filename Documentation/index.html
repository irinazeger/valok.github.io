Steps to make contact form working

Do below changes in "mailer.php" file to receive mail for the form filled in contact.html

Line 6: In place of "xxxxxxxxxxxx" add your AWS or email username.
Line 7: In place of "xxxxxxxxxxxx" add your AWS or email password.
Line 8: In place of "abc@example.com" add your from email id.
Line 9: In place of "Bloom inn" add your from name via which name you want to send email.
Line 11: In place of "def@example.com" add your to email id where you want to receive form lead details.
Line 12: In place of "ghi@example.com" add your cc email id if in case you want one lead copy to be receive on another email ID too.
Line 13: In place of "xyz@example.com" add your bcc email id if in case you want one lead copy to be receive on another email ID too.
Line 14: In place of "Contact form" add your subject line as per your requirement.
Line 16: In place of "email-smtp.us-east-1.amazonaws.com" you can add your own host.
Line 17: In place of "xxx" you can change it to your port setting.
Line 19: In place of "Bloom inn - Thank you for contacting us!" you can change it to your confirmation email subject line.

Customize confirmation email message

Do below changes in "thanks.html" file to customize confirmation email message

Line 15: Add full logo URL.
Line 33: Add your confirmation email message.

Check Availability Form Structure

Bloom Inn uses simple and unique check availability system which is time saving. You can check for the availability by just clicking on the flexible day & room you prefer. Our Check availability system provide stay for the customers based on the room availability. Moreover, they avoid two customer booking same room at same date.
